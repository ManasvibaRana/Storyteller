Thriller

Jumper

"JUMPER" is a suspense thriller short film that follows a businesswoman who, after a peculiar encounter in an elevator, becomes convinced that she is being followed. The film stars Madison Reimer as the businesswoman and Randy Swanson as the janitor.

Koi Ane Ko Hai

"Koi Aane Ko Hai" is a thriller short film set in a real cemetery. The story follows a night security guard who assists a lone girl in crossing the cemetery late at night to reach the train station. As they navigate the eerie surroundings, unexpected and unsettling events unfold, leading to a suspenseful and chilling experience.

The Perfect Murder

"The Perfect Murder" is an award-winning crime drama short film written and directed by Vikkramm Chandirramani. The narrative delves into the complexities of urban crime, presenting a gripping story that has garnered critical acclaim and numerous awards. The film's compelling storytelling and direction have been widely praised.

Violent Killer

The video titled "Australia's Most Heartless Child Killer | True Crime Stories in 10 Mins!" delves into the life and crimes of Stewart John Regan, one of Australia's most violent criminals. The narrative chronicles his early years, marked by animal cruelty, and his progression into more heinous acts, culminating in murder. The video provides a concise yet comprehensive overview of Regan's actions and the impact they had.
