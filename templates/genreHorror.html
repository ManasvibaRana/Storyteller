Horror

The Scarecrow

"The Scarecrow" is a short horror film that delves into the eerie events surrounding a seemingly ordinary scarecrow. As the narrative unfolds, viewers are drawn into a suspenseful tale where the scarecrow becomes central to a series of unsettling occurrences. The film masterfully builds tension, leading to a chilling climax that leaves a lasting impression.

The Elevator

"The Elevator" is a horror short film that follows a girl who becomes trapped in a malevolent elevator. As she descends through floors of darkness, a vindictive voice demands something from her, leading to a chilling experience.

Toothbrush

"Toothbrush" is a horror short film featuring Lalit Prabhakar. The story follows a man who, after moving into his new home, encounters a disturbing incident involving a toothbrush. The film delves into themes of isolation and the supernatural, creating a suspenseful atmosphere.

Double Vision

"Double Vision" is an independent horror short film that follows a girl who, on her way home, begins experiencing unsettling visions. Upon returning home, she confronts the eerie phenomena she encountered earlier. The film delves into themes of perception and reality, creating a suspenseful narrative that keeps viewers on edge.
