Plus Minus

"Plus Minus" is an award-winning short film featuring Divya Dutta and Bhuvan Bam. The narrative revolves around two strangers who, during a shared journey, engage in conversations that lead them to reflect on their personal lives and relationships. Through their interaction, the film delves into themes of human connection and the impact of perspective. Notably, "Plus Minus" won the Filmfare Award in 2019 for Best Short Film (People's Choice Category)

Post It

"Post It" is a short film that delves into the dynamics between Elroy, who desires solitude, and Clara, whose persistent attempts to connect challenge his isolation. Set in a confined space, the narrative explores themes of personal boundaries, human connection, and the impact of uninvited interactions. The film offers a nuanced look at how individuals respond to unwanted attention and the complexities of social engagement..
